About Us


JJ Finance is a web application through which users can simulate trading in the real world. It is a great tool for beginners trying to familiarize themselves with stock trading before taking on any real risk. It is also a great platform to test investing strategies and gain experience without spending a single penny. Users can virtually buy and sell stocks from the National Stock Exchange of India and create their own portfolio. They can also create a watchlist of stocks to keep an eye on.

Developed and created by Siddhartha Jaiswal and Aryan Jain
